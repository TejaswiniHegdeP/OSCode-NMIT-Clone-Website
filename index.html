About Us

OScode is a student-led open-source club at NMIT, fostering learning, collaboration, and innovation

We help students explore open-source contributions, work on real-world projects, and prepare for platforms like Google Summer of Code (GSoC).

Our community brings like-minded developers together to learn, build, and grow through hands-on experience.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, OScode provides the perfect space to enhance your skills and advance your career.

Hands on learning

Gain practical experience by working on real-world open source projects. Learn by doing, contribute code, and collaborate with the community. Develop your skills through interactive coding, problem-solving, and project-based learning

Mentorship by experts

Learn from experienced open source contributors and industry leaders. Get personalized guidance, improve your coding skills, and navigate your open-source journey with expert mentorship. Gain insights, receive feedback, and grow with hands-on support from seasoned professionals.

Hackathons & Challenges

Compete in global hackathons, solve real-world problems, and collaborate on open-source projects. Gain hands-on experience, learn new technologies, and showcase your skills in a dynamic, engaging environment
